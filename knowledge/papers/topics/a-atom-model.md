# A-atom (Average Atom) Model

**Concept:** Single pseudo-element replacing all constituents of a random alloy, with concentration-weighted averaged EAM potential parameters.

**Key papers:** Nohring & Curtin (2016), Zeller et al. (2025), Pasianot & Farkas (2020)

**LAMMPS support:** No native tool -- requires external generation of averaged funcfl/setfl file.

**Limitations:** Underestimates strengthening, misses local chemical fluctuations.

**See:** `knowledge/papers/notes/a-atom-model.md` for full guidance.
